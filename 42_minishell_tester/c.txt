usage: mstest [m,vm,ne,b,a] {builtins,b,parsing,pa,redirections,r,pipelines,pi,cmds,c,variables,v,corrections,co,path,syntax,s}...
m: mandatory tests
vm: mandatory tests with valgrind
ne: tests without environment
b: bonus tests
a: mandatory and bonus tests
d: mandatory pipe segfault test (BRUTAL)
Starting from the second argument, their can be any number of argument specified between brackets.
You can test a specific part of your minishell by writing mstest vm builtins redirections
If the part list is empty, everything will be tested.
